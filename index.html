Loan Eligibility Calculator

This loan eligibility calculator helps you estimate how much loan you may be eligible for based on your monthly income and EMI affordability.

Banks and lenders usually allow a certain percentage of your monthly income to be used for loan repayment. This calculator gives a quick estimate.

How Loan Eligibility is Calculated

Loan eligibility is usually calculated as a percentage of your monthly income. Most banks allow 30% to 50% of your income to be used as EMI.

Eligible EMI = Monthly Income × Eligibility Percentage

Example of Loan Eligibility Calculation

If your monthly income is ₹50,000 and the EMI affordability is 40%, then your eligible EMI will be:

Eligible EMI = 50,000 × 40% = ₹20,000
